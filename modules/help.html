TYRIAN LENS - v1.2.4
GETTING STARTED & SETTINGS ▼

API Keys

Tyrian Lens relies on the official Guild Wars 2 API to display your account data securely. Go to the API Keys module to add your key.

DRF Live Loot Tracking

To enable real-time loot drops in the Farming Tracker, you need the DRF addon.

Settings

In the Settings, you can adjust UI scale, volume, and default character.

MAP & TIMERS ▼

Map Options

Tyrian Lens automatically detects if you are in Tyria or WvW. Toggle layers via Map Options.

Timers & Notifications

The Timers module tracks World Bosses and Meta Events. Click the X-Ray icon to add events to your Watchlist.

ACCOUNT SEARCH & STORAGE ▼

Account Search

Scan your Bank, Material Storage, and Character Bags for items and currencies.

Bank, Materials & Wardrobe

Browse account-wide storage. Click items to open their official Wiki page.

CHARACTERS & HERO PANEL ▼

Hero Panel

View equipped gear, weapons, and builds. Tooltips update automatically based on your selection.

Character Roster & Inventory

Overview your roster playtime, deaths, and birthdays. Check bags without logging into characters.

TRADING & ECONOMY ▼

Trading Post & Deliveries

Track Buy/Sell orders. The TP icon glows when items are waiting in your Delivery Box.

Smart Crafting

Calculates crafting profit based on your actual Material Storage and market prices.

PROGRESSION & TRACKING ▼

Achievements

Search achievements and add them to your permanent on-screen Watchlist Overlay.

Weekly Clears

Visual dashboard for Raids, Strike Missions, and Dungeons.

Farming Tracker

Snapshots your wallet and inventory to calculate gains during farming sessions.
