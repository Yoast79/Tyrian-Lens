TYRIAN LENS - v1.1.3
GETTING STARTED & SETTINGS ▼

API Keys

Tyrian Lens relies on the official Guild Wars 2 API to display your account data securely. Go to the API Keys module to add your key. We recommend selecting all permissions when generating your key on the ArenaNet website to ensure all modules (like Account Search and Clears) function correctly.

Settings

In the Settings, you can adjust the UI scale, toggle the overlay's "Always on Top" behavior, adjust the volume of timer notifications, and select a default character to load in the Hero Panel.

MAP & TIMERS ▼

Map Options

Tyrian Lens automatically detects if you are in Tyria or WvW and switches the map accordingly. You can toggle map layers (Regions, Sectors, Names) via Map Options. Most map icons can be clicked to copy the chat-link directly to your clipboard.

Timers & Notifications

The Timers module tracks World Bosses, Meta Events, and Day/Night cycles. Click the next to any event to add it to your Watchlist. You will receive an on-screen popup and an audio chime before the event starts, and exactly when it begins.

ACCOUNT SEARCH & STORAGE ▼

Account Search

Lost an item? Type at least two letters in the Account Search module to scan your entire account. It locates items in your Bank, Material Storage, Shared Inventory, and specific Character Bags. It also searches your Wallet for currencies!

Bank, Materials & Wardrobe

Browse your account-wide storage. You can hover over any item to see its description and stats, or click the item to open its official Guild Wars 2 Wiki page in your browser.

CHARACTERS & HERO PANEL ▼

Hero Panel

View the equipped gear, weapons, and build of any character. Hovering over traits and skills will generate a detailed tooltip just like in the game. Items display their applied runes, sigils, and infusions. The attributes update automatically to the selected weapons.

Character Roster & Inventory

The Characters module gives you an overview of your entire roster, including total playtime, deaths, and next birthdays. Use the Inventory module to quickly check the specific bag contents of any character without logging into them.

TRADING & ECONOMY ▼

Trading Post & Deliveries

Track your current Buy/Sell orders and transaction history. The TP icon in the main menu will show a if you have items or coins waiting in your Delivery Box.

Smart Crafting

This tool analyzes your actual Material Storage and compares it to live market prices. It calculates the exact cost to buy missing materials and shows you your True Net Profit for crafting time-gated items (like Deldrimor Steel). You can add custom items to this list via the Market Search.

Market Search & Investments

Search for any item on the TP. Click it to view current Buy/Sell spreads. Click Track Investment to log a purchase. Tyrian Lens will monitor the item's value over time, factor in the 15% TP tax, and draw a profit graph for your portfolio.

PROGRESSION & TRACKING ▼

Achievements

Search the massive GW2 achievement database. Click an achievement to add it to your Watchlist Overlay. This creates a permanent, auto-updating tracker on the right side of your screen while you play.

Weekly Clears

A visual dashboard tracking your progress in Raids, Strike Missions, and Dungeons. It automatically syncs with the Guild Wars 2 weekly reset, changing from "Cleared" back to "Uncleared" when the week rolls over.

Farming Tracker

Start a session before doing a meta train or farming run. The tracker takes a snapshot of your wallet and inventory. When you stop the session, it calculates your exact gains in gold, karma, currencies, and loot. The tracker icon will glow green when it is actively tracking your gains. (Note: It normalizes data, so moving an item from your bag to your bank isn't counted as "new loot").
